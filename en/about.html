Who's PulsAid?

What Do We Do?

PulsAid is a groundbreaking initiative with one mission:
to increase survival rates during cardiac arrests.

By quickly mobilizing our network of trained citizen responders, PulsAid aims to significantly enhance survival rates in Belgium, saving more lives and minimizing the impact of cardiac arrests.

Be part of the change. Join PulsAid and contribute to shaping heart care in Belgium. Together, we can make a difference.

What Are Citizen Responders?

Citizen responders play a crucial role in emergencies. These are ordinary citizens trained to provide first aid until professional emergency services arrive. Citizen responders are ready to save lives, and their quick response, basic knowledge of CPR, and the use of defibrillators make them valuable links in the chain of emergency care.
