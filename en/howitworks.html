How it works

Step 1: Receive a Notification

As soon as an emergency occurs, we ensure that you are immediately informed. Through our advanced notification systems, you receive timely and accurate information about the situation. Step 1 is essential for a quick and effective response, so we are ready to provide you with the information you need to take action and make a difference. Together, we contribute to a safer community, step by step.

Step 2: Find an AED

Once you receive the notification, we assist you in locating the nearest Automated External Defibrillator (AED). This crucial step significantly increases the chances of successful resuscitation. Our app effortlessly guides you to the AED's location, enabling you to act quickly and save lives. In Step 2, we harness the power of technology to ensure help is within reach when every second counts.

Step 3: After Resuscitation

After the intense experience of resuscitation, we understand that emotional support is crucial. Our app goes beyond the physical actions and also provides assistance for the emotional impact. Step 3 focuses on providing reassurance, guidance, and tools for those involved in the resuscitation. Together, we work towards restoring well-being and resilience, ensuring those who helped feel supported on both physical and emotional levels. In challenging moments, we stand side by side, from resuscitation to emotional recovery.
