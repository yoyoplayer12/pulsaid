PulsAid

With a targeted approach, we aim to intervene more quickly in cases of heart failure and simultaneously significantly enhance survival rates. Our commitment lies in optimizing response mechanisms, resulting in a new network that can act immediately and thus minimize the impact of heart failure.

9 000

Cardiac arrests in Belgium

Why?

Because every second counts, and together we can make a difference that changes lives. We believe in transforming emergency situations into hopeful moments. Our mission is clear: to increase survival chances during cardiac arrests. With a network of trained responders, we mobilize quickly, save more lives, and minimize the impact.
